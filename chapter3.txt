Chapter 3: Unveiling the Past

As Elara delved deeper into the mansion's mysteries, she uncovered a forgotten love story between two spirits bound to the estate. Their tragic tale echoed through the halls, trapped in a curse that only Elara could break. Determined to free them, she embarked on a quest to collect artifacts scattered throughout the mansion. Each artifact revealed a piece of the spirits' story, bringing Elara closer to unraveling the curse and discovering the mansion's true purpose.
